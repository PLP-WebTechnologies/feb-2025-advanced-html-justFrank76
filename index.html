What is FNM Studios?

FNM Studios is an institution that trains potential photographers and videographers the skills and the art of taking photos and making videos like a professional.

We strive to train learners and equip them with skills that will help them in becoming the best in the film industry.

Currently we have five center in the country which are located in:

  1. Nairobi
  2. Mombasa
  3. Kisumu
  4. Eldoret
  5. Nakuru

Why join FNM Studios?

FNM Studios have a team of trainers who have been successful in the film industry ensuring that learners learn from the best in the game. We also have cutting edge technology in the film industry, ensuring learners are to-date with the latest trends in photography and videography.

We offer the following in our institutions:

  1. Photography
  2. Videography
  3. Script writing
  4. Video editing
